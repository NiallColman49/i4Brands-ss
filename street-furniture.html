Street Furniture and Metal Fabrication

From Design, to Development all the way through to Delivery. With years of experience in the industry, i4brands are recognised for high quality products through our specialist partners. Our range of street furniture enhances any scheme or project.

Our extensive street furniture products include anti-ram raid security bollards, Street signage, external litter bins, internal recycling units, seating & benches, planters, tree protection, canopies, smoking point and bus stop shelters.

Our range of street furniture products are available in a variety of styles and finishes. From classic and traditional to contemporary designs, we have a huge choice available. They suit any application and integrate into the surrounding environment. Our furniture provides both functional and aesthetic benefits. Our high quality street furniture products are made with both function and style in mind. Whichever style you choose, it is always fit for purpose. Our extensive street furniture range provides architects and specifiers with both inspiration and a wide choice. We can adapt items to meet bespoke requirements. Items can also be customised to include logos or names.
